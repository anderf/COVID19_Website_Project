Prevention Tips:

There's currently no vaccine to prevent COVID-19 disease.

You can protect yourself and help prevent spreading the virus to others if you:

  1. Find Local Information
    • Know where to find local information on COVID-19 and local trends of COVID-19 cases



  2. Stay home if you can
    • Stay home when you are sick
    • Call your health care provider’s office in advance of a visit
    • Limit movement in the community and visitors



  3. Clean your hands often
    • Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds especially after you have been in public place or after blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing
    • If soap and water are not readily available, use a hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol


  4. Have a emergence plan
    • Know about emergency operations plans for schools/workplaces of household members
